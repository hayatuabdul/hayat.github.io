About Me
My journey into the world of Data Engineering and Data Science began during my undergraduate studies, where I pursued a Bachelor's degree in Robotics. I was captivated by the possibilities that AI offered in analyzing vast amounts of data and uncovering patterns that could lead to groundbreaking discoveries. Throughout my academic journey, I delved into various AI algorithms, machine learning techniques, and data-driven problem-solving, laying a strong foundation for my future endeavors.

Continuing my passion for AI, I pursued a Master's degree in the same field, further refining my skills and knowledge. During my postgraduate studies, I focused on advanced topics such as deep learning, natural language processing, and reinforcement learning, broadening my understanding of cutting-edge AI technologies.

Path to the Career of Data and AI
The combination of my educational background in AI and my work experience as a Digital Analyst paved the way for my transition into the field of Data Engineering and Data Science. I realized that the key to making a significant impact lies in the seamless integration of data-driven solutions with cutting-edge AI technologies.

Embracing this vision, I furthered my journey by immersing myself in the world of Data Engineering. I honed my skills in designing and building robust data pipelines, ensuring the efficient extraction, transformation, and loading of data from various sources. Leveraging technologies like Apache Spark and AWS Cloud platform, I focused on creating scalable solutions to handle large-scale data processing and analysis.

As a Data Scientist, I found joy in exploring data, uncovering hidden patterns, and creating predictive models. My experience as a Digital Analyst taught me the value of actionable insights, and I applied this knowledge in developing data-driven solutions for businesses. Whether it's predicting customer churn, recommending personalized content, or optimizing supply chain operations, I am dedicated to delivering data-centric solutions that make a meaningful difference.
